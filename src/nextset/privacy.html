Privacy Policy

NextSet - Gym Timer App

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to NextSet ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application NextSet handles your information.

Key Point: NextSet is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Information We Collect

NextSet does not collect any personal information. The app functions entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device.

The only data stored by the app includes:

  • Workout set configurations (name, number of sets, duration)
  • Your notification preferences
  • Usage timestamps for sorting your recently used sets

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard device storage mechanisms. This means:

  • Your data remains on your device only
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all associated data
  • We cannot access, view, or recover your data

Third-Party Services

NextSet does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information in the first place.

Children's Privacy

NextSet does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Permissions

NextSet may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when your workout set or break is about to end

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
